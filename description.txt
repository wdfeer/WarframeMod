Adds stuff from Warframe

153 items:
- 85 Weapons
- 66 Accessories (including 19 Arcanes and 5 Auras)
- 2 Materials

4 new Debuffs available via the mod's items: Bleed, Electro, Cold, Weak

Gameplay changes:
- Over 100% Crits from Warframe, hit colors configurable
- Extra accessory slot for Arcanes, expert-exclusive
  (enemies have higher stats to compensate: +5% life, defense and damage by default, configurable)
- Vanilla weapons have increased crit chance (10% by default, configurable)

Note that some items' effects are not the same as in Warframe
